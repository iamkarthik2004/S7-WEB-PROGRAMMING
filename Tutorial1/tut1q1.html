S7 CSE SUBJECTS

Semester 7 (S7) of the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program under APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) marks a critical transition into advanced, industry-relevant computing principles and the foundational stages of career placement. According to the KTU 2019 Scheme Curriculum, students tackle highly applicable core papers alongside multiple professional electives, transitioning from foundational programming to emerging domains like Artificial Intelligence (CST401), Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, and Web Programming. In addition to intensive theory modules, S7 places a heavy emphasis on practical execution, featuring a specialized industry lab, a mandatory technical seminar to hone presentation and research skills, and the crucial launch of the Phase 1 Capstone Project Work, which sets the baseline for their final evaluation in S8.

S8 CSE Subjects

Semester 8 (S8) for Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) under the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) acts as the final, career-launching phase of the B.Tech program. Designed with a lighter classroom load to accommodate heavy project demands, the curriculum focuses heavily on technical specialization and industry readiness. According to the standard KTU 2019 Scheme, students tackle advanced core theory like Distributed Computing alongside targeted domain knowledge through Program Electives. These electives allow students to customize their final semester by choosing cutting-edge pathways such as Deep Learning, Cloud Computing, Embedded Systems, or Data Mining.
